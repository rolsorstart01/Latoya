A CULINARY JOURNEY THROUGH LATIN AMERICA

Our Story

Latoya, a vibrant Latin American restaurant and bar, invites you on a culinary journey that blends the region's rich heritage with diverse global influences. Our menu offers a fusion of flavors, from Peru's Japanese-inspired cuisine to the Caribbean's Indian-spiced dishes, showcasing Latin America's culinary versatility.

Our handcrafted cocktails, made with fresh and unique ingredients, are as visually striking as they are delicious. At Latoya, dining is more than just a meal—it's an experience. With a warm atmosphere and exceptional service, we invite you to explore the stories behind every dish.
